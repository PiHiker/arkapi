How the marketplace works
Three steps to monetize your API.
You register an endpoint, set your price in sats, and point it at your server. ArkAPI proxies every request, handles the payment, and forwards the sats to your wallet.
01
📡

Register

Submit your API endpoint, set a price per call in sats, and provide your backend URL. We generate a public route on ArkAPI that consumers can discover and call.

02
⚡

We proxy

When a consumer calls your endpoint, ArkAPI authenticates the session, checks their balance, deducts sats, and forwards the request to your backend. You never touch payments.

03
₿

Earn sats

Your share of every call is accumulated and settled to your Bitcoin wallet via Lightning or Ark. You set the price, we take a small platform fee, and the rest is yours.

What you provide

  • ✓ An HTTP endpoint that accepts JSON and returns JSON
  • ✓ A price per call in sats (you choose)
  • ✓ A Bitcoin wallet address for payouts (Lightning or Ark)
  • ✓ A short description and category for the catalog listing
  • ✓ Uptime — keep your server running and responding

What we handle

  • ✓ Session-based auth and bearer token management
  • ✓ Bitcoin payment collection via Ark and Lightning
  • ✓ Per-call metering and balance deduction
  • ✓ Rate limiting and abuse protection
  • ✓ Catalog listing, discovery, and API documentation
  • ✓ Automatic sats settlement to your wallet
